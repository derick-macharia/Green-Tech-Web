At X-Solution Irrigation Sacco Company, we offer a variety of irrigation and agricultural services to meet the diverse needs of modern farming. Each service is tailored to optimize efficiency, reduce costs, and support sustainable farming practices, among them are:

  1. Custom Irrigation System Design & Installation-

    X-Solution Irrigation Sacco provides custom-designed irrigation systems tailored to your farm’s needs, ensuring water efficiency and optimal crop growth. Our expert installation ensures reliable, long-lasting systems that meet your specific requirements.

  2. Irrigation System Maintenance & Repair

    We offer prompt maintenance and repair services for irrigation systems, addressing issues like leaks and malfunctions to ensure consistent water delivery and system performance, minimizing disruptions to your crops.

  3. Water & Fertigation Management

    Our water and fertigation management services optimize water and nutrient usage, ensuring crops receive the right amount of irrigation and fertilizers at the right time, improving efficiency and reducing waste.

  4. Agricultural Consultation & Farm Design

    We provide expert consultation on farm design and management, offering tailored advice on crop selection and layout to enhance productivity, sustainability, and profitability for your farm.

  5. Smart & Automated Irrigation Solutions

    We design and install smart, automated irrigation systems that adjust based on soil moisture and weather, ensuring efficient water use while cutting down on labor and waste.

  6. Rainwater Harvesting & Sustainable Irrigation

    Our rainwater harvesting systems collect and store rainwater for irrigation, reducing reliance on traditional water sources, cutting costs, and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.
